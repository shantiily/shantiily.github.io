Media and Information Design Framework

Title: AI's Growing Influence: What It Means for Us

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly changing the way we live and work. In healthcare, AI tools now diagnose diseases with high accuracy, improving patient outcomes. The financial industry uses AI to predict market trends and detect fraud, making transactions safer.

AI's convenience is evident in daily life, from personalized content recommendations to virtual assistants. However, its rise also brings concerns about job displacement and ethical issues.
Experts emphasize the importance of responsible AI use and preparing for the future through education and skill development. As AI continues to evolve, its potential to enhance our lives is immense—if managed wisely.

Project Title: Media's Influence on Daily Life

In today's world, media shapes how we experience and interpret reality. This project explores how constant media exposure influences our daily lives, focusing on a young woman absorbed in her phone. Surrounded by diverse opinions and perspectives, she becomes immersed in an alternate reality crafted by media. This phenomenon affects her worldview, shaping her thoughts and behaviors, and reveals how media can create a parallel life that impacts our understanding of the real world.
